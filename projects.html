CipherVault

CipherVault is a password storage and encryption system written in C++ as my final project of the CS-100 course in my first year at LUMS.

It can store multiple users and generate an encryption key from their usernames, allowing them to store all their passwords in one place with the need to only remember the password to their CipherVault account!
